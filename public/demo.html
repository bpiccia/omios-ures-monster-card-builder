Integration Instructions

To integrate this into your website:

  1. Include React and ReactDOM in your page
  2. Include the built bundle: omios-uries-card-maker.js
  3. Call window.OmiosUriesCardMaker.init() to open the modal
<script crossorigin src="https://unpkg.com/react@18/umd/react.production.min.js"></script>
<script crossorigin src="https://unpkg.com/react-dom@18/umd/react-dom.production.min.js"></script>
<script src="./dist/omios-uries-card-maker.js"></script>

<button onclick="window.OmiosUriesCardMaker.init()">
    Open Monster Card Maker
</button>
